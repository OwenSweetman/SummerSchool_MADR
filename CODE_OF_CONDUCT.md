# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as its code of conduct.

All contributors, maintainers, and participants in project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions, and related community channels) are expected to follow it.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, and also applies when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Reporting

Instances of unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project maintainers by opening a confidential issue, or by contacting a maintainer directly. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of anyone reporting an incident.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing the standards of acceptable behaviour. They will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behaviour that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful. The full enforcement guidelines are described in the linked Contributor Covenant document.
